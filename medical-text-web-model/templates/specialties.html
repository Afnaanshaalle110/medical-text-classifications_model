Cardiology

Heart and cardiovascular system

About Cardiology

Cardiology is a branch of medicine that deals with disorders of the heart and cardiovascular system. Cardiologists are specialists who diagnose, assess, and treat patients with diseases and defects of the heart and blood vessels.

Common Conditions

  • Coronary artery disease
  • Heart failure
  • Arrhythmias
  • Valvular heart disease
  • Hypertension
  • Congenital heart defects

Keywords for Classification

heart chest pain ecg ekg cardiac arrhythmia palpitation hypertension blood pressure coronary atrial fibrillation myocardial st elevation

Orthopedics

Musculoskeletal system and bones

About Orthopedics

Orthopedics is the branch of medicine concerned with the study and treatment of the musculoskeletal system, particularly the spine, joints, and muscles. Orthopedic surgeons use both surgical and nonsurgical approaches to treat musculoskeletal trauma, sports injuries, degenerative diseases, infections, tumors, and congenital disorders.

Common Conditions

  • Arthritis
  • Osteoporosis
  • Fractures
  • Joint pain
  • Spine disorders
  • Sports injuries

Keywords for Classification

bone joint fracture sprain arthritis knee hip osteoarthritis x-ray cast orthopedic spine vertebra

Neurology

Brain and nervous system

About Neurology

Neurology is the branch of medicine dealing with the diagnosis and treatment of all categories of conditions and diseases involving the central and peripheral nervous systems. Neurologists manage and treat neurological conditions, or problems with the nervous system.

Common Conditions

  • Headaches and migraines
  • Epilepsy
  • Stroke
  • Multiple sclerosis
  • Parkinson's disease
  • Alzheimer's disease

Keywords for Classification

brain headache migraine seizure neurological stroke visual aura nerve cognitive memory tremor parkinson

Pediatrics

Children's health

About Pediatrics

Pediatrics is the branch of medicine that involves the medical care of infants, children, and adolescents. Pediatricians work to reduce infant and child mortality, control infectious disease, foster healthy lifestyles, and ease the difficulties of children with chronic conditions.

Common Conditions

  • Respiratory infections
  • Ear infections
  • Developmental disorders
  • Childhood vaccinations
  • Growth issues
  • Childhood diseases

Keywords for Classification

child infant pediatric fever vaccination growth developmental tonsil ear infection rash cough

Dermatology

Skin, hair, and nails

About Dermatology

Dermatology is the branch of medicine dealing with the skin, nails, hair and its diseases. It is a specialty with both medical and surgical aspects. A dermatologist is a specialist doctor who manages diseases related to skin, hair, nails, and some cosmetic problems.

Common Conditions

  • Acne
  • Eczema
  • Psoriasis
  • Skin cancer
  • Dermatitis
  • Hair loss

Keywords for Classification

skin rash dermatitis acne eczema psoriasis mole itching melanoma dermatological

General Surgery

Surgical procedures

About General Surgery

General surgery is a surgical specialty that focuses on abdominal contents including the esophagus, stomach, small intestine, large intestine, liver, pancreas, gallbladder, appendix and bile ducts, and often the thyroid gland. General surgeons perform a wide range of procedures and often deal with emergency situations.

Common Procedures

  • Appendectomy
  • Cholecystectomy (gallbladder removal)
  • Hernia repair
  • Colon surgery
  • Laparoscopic procedures
  • Trauma surgery

Keywords for Classification

surgery appendicitis gallbladder hernia appendix abdominal pain surgical incision laparoscopic
